Technical Requirements

Device

A desktop or laptop computer with the ability to access the internet. Chromebooks are supported, but it is recommended to use a Windows/Mac/Linux/etc. if possible.

Programs

You can use any* IDE of your choice. You need to be able to open PDFs locally on your device. You will also need to be able to extract .7z archives, either with a program like 7-Zip or an online service such as https://extract.me/.

*Please do not use any web-based IDEs other than the one provided via HackerRank; platforms such as Replit and Ideone make your code publicly visible, which is a rules violation.

Internet

An internet connection is required to access the HackerRank platform and submit your solutions. Problems statements will be downloaded onto your computer, so you can continue working in the case of an outage.

HackerRank Account

You must register for a personal HackerRank account before the contest begins. Register for a free account at https://www.hackerrank.com/dashboard.
